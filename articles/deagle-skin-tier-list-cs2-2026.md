**What is the best Desert Eagle skin in CS2?**
Desert Eagle | Blaze is widely considered the best Deagle skin — iconic design, limited wear conditions, and strong long-term value. For budget players, the Printstream in Field-Tested at $35 is the best value pick.

**Is the Desert Eagle | Printstream worth buying?**

**Does Desert Eagle skin wear affect gameplay?**
No — skin wear only affects visual appearance, never in-game performance. The Desert Eagle itself performs identically regardless of what skin is equipped.

**What is the rarest Desert Eagle skin in CS2?**

**Which Desert Eagle skin has the best pattern variation?**
